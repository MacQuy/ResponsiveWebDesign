Features

Borrelsword Dragon isn’t just another Link Monster—it’s a relentless finisher designed for decisive duels.

  • High Impact ATK: Starts with 3000 ATK, capable of overpowering most monsters.
  • Double Attack Potential: Change a monster to defense position and attack again—perfect for OTK (One Turn Kill) setups.
  • Battle Immunity: This card cannot be destroyed by battle, making it a persistent threat on the field.
  • Field Control: Disrupts your opponent’s battle phase by bypassing their strongest monsters and finishing the game quickly.

Whether you're playing in casual duels or competitive tournaments, Borrelsword gives you the edge you need.
